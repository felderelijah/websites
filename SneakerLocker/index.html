About

This website focus will be to provide information to the sneaker community about sneaker events/releases, brands, discord sneaker community channels, and sneaker influencers.

The intended audience for this website is everyone. All individuals who are interested or passionate about footwear ranging from sneakers and athletic wear.

The information that I want viewers to take away from this website are release dates, story behind the shoes, colorways, and pricing.
